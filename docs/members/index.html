Mariane Weber

Max Weber

Max Weber (1864–1920) was a pioneering German sociologist, philosopher, and political economist, best known for his profound impact on the study of sociology and his theories on capitalism, bureaucracy, and religion. Born in Erfurt, Prussia, Weber grew up in an intellectually vibrant environment, which significantly influenced his scholarly pursuits.
